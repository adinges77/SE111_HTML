Review of Sinister 1

By Anton Dinges August 18, 2022

This movie is about a crime writer who decided to move into a house that had murders happen there. He ends up finding films of the past families being killed recorded by one of the family members doing the killing. During the movie one of the kids begin to change which happens from the bag guy in the movie Bughuul.

Trivia
  • This movie was inspired by The Ring which I did not even know.
  • The script was written only in five weeks.
  • The scene in the movie showing the super-8 home videos were filmed on a real super-8 camera.
